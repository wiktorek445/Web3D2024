About Popular Drinks

Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola, also known as Coke, was invented by John Stith Pemberton in 1886. Originally intended as a patent medicine, it was sold at soda fountains in the United States. Over the years, Coca-Cola has become one of the most popular and recognizable brands worldwide.

Sprite

Sprite was introduced by The Coca-Cola Company in 1961 as a competitor to 7 Up. Known for its crisp and refreshing lemon-lime flavor, Sprite has become a leading lemon-lime soda worldwide. It is also recognized for its vibrant green branding and memorable advertising campaigns.

Pepsi

Pepsi, originally known as Brad's Drink, was created by Caleb Bradham in 1893. It was later renamed Pepsi-Cola in 1898. Pepsi has grown into one of the world's largest soft drink brands, famous for its refreshing taste and iconic blue, red, and white logo.
